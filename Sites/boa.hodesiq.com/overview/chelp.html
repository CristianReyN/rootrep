Guided Job Search - State/City or Zip Code/Distance Search

 close window
  • When searching by location, you must choose to search by defining a specific state AND city OR zip code AND distance. If you choose state/city and zip code/distance location options, your results will only return jobs based on the designated zip code/distance.
  • City or zip code search options are limited to areas in which we currently have available job opportunities. These locations change and are updated constantly. If your city of choice is not listed, you may choose to search another city or zip code/distance or check back at a later date.
  • State/City search results are specific to the single city you select, and do not include results for nearby locations.
  • If you are looking for opportunities within a nearby location, enter a specific zip code and the specified distance you wish to include.
    For example: if you choose to search for opportunities within 20 miles of Jackson Heights (NY), you will enter the zip code of 11372 and the distance of 20 miles. Your search results will include available opportunities within Astoria, Jamaica, Queens, Brooklyn, New York City as well as other nearby locations within a 20 mile radius.
